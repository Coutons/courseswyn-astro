## Limitations to Know

**No Direct Purchases**
You can't buy courses inside ChatGPT. Enrollment links redirect to Udemy's website.

**English Only (For Now)**
Initial launch supports English with subtitle options. More languages planned.

**Course Quality Varies**
ChatGPT recommends based on ratings and relevance, but Udemy courses have inconsistent quality. Always check recent reviews before enrolling.

**Data Privacy**
Your ChatGPT conversations may inform course recommendations. Review OpenAI's privacy policy if concerned.

## FAQ

### Is Udemy ChatGPT integration free?

Yes, the integration is free for all ChatGPT users (Free, Go, Plus, Pro). However, Udemy courses themselves require payment.

### Do I need a Udemy account to browse courses in ChatGPT?

No. You can explore course recommendations without logging into Udemy. Account required only for enrollment.

### Can I watch full courses in ChatGPT?

You can watch course previews and some videos in-chat, but full course access requires enrollment on Udemy's platform.

### Are Udemy courses cheaper through ChatGPT?

No. Prices match Udemy's standard pricing. Use coupon sites for discounts before purchasing.

### Which courses does ChatGPT recommend?

ChatGPT recommends based on course ratings, student count, instructor credibility, and relevance to your query. Popular courses with high ratings appear more often.

### Can I use ChatGPT to compare Udemy vs Coursera?

Yes. ChatGPT can help compare platforms based on available information, but the Udemy app integration provides deeper course data specifically for Udemy.

### Does this work on mobile ChatGPT?

Yes, available on ChatGPT mobile app for iOS and Android.

### Will my chat history affect course recommendations?

Potentially. ChatGPT uses conversation context to suggest relevant courses, but doesn't store personal learning profiles unless you're logged into Udemy within ChatGPT.
