What is AI Art Generation?

AI art generation is the process of creating art and images using artificial intelligence (AI). This technique entails using AI algorithms and machine learning to help produce art and image (like Midjourney and Stable Diffusion). AI art generators are trained on large image datasets to learn the correlations between colors, forms, and other visual components. As a result, they can develop new sets of images, such as new artworks, imitate existing styles, or add artistic features to images.
