Welcome to Design Marketing Content with AI in Adobe Express– a project-based course where you’ll learn how to use AI tools to create professional branding, video content, animated characters, and high-impact social media graphics.

Whether you run a small business, manage social content, or want to level up your design skills, this course will show you how to design eye-catching marketing materials quickly and confidently using Adobe Express (free or paid).

You’ll learn how to use the latest AI design tools – including Adobe Firefly, ChatGPT, Google Labs Whisk, and Gemini Flash (aka Nano Banana) – to generate graphics, build brand identity, and create compelling motion content. By the end, you’ll be designing marketing visuals that look polished, modern, and attention-grabbing across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn, and beyond.

What You’ll Learn

  • How to create animated characters and avatars for branding
  • How to generate logos, icons, type styles and brand kits using AI tools
  • Using ChatGPT & Firefly to create graphics, promotional imagery & content ideas
  • Building colour palettes, font pairings & mood boards for strong branding
  • Creating impressive AI video intros using effective prompting
  • Enhancing voiceovers using AI audio tools
  • Designing scroll-stopping social media posts optimized for multiple platforms
  • Working with image cutouts, text, video, graphics & templates
  • Creating a cohesive brand identity that stands out
  • Every section includes a hands-on project, so you learn by doing – and finish with a set of visual marketing assets you can actually use.

Who This Course Is For

Hi, I’m Tim – an Adobe Certified Instructor, Adobe Certified Expert, and creative designer based in London.

This course is ideal for:

  • Small business owners and entrepreneurs
  • Content creators, influencers & social media managers
  • Students & career-builders looking to add AI-powered design skills
  • Home users who want creative projects made easy

If you’re brand new to Adobe Express, you can follow along easily – I cover the interface early on. If you already know the basics, feel free to skip ahead.

Tools We’ll Use

You’ll explore powerful AI and branding tools including:

  • Adobe Firefly for text & image generation
  • ChatGPT and Gemini Flash for concept development and prompts
  • Google Labs Whisk for advanced animation from portraits
  • Adobe Express Brand Kits, video effects & voice tools
  • Auto cutout and template-based editing

Short, clear video lessons ensure rapid learning and fast results – even if you’ve never designed before.

What You Need

By the End of the Course
