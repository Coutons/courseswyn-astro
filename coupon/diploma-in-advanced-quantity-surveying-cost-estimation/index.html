What You Will Learn

By the end of this course, you will confidently master:

1. Quantity Surveying Fundamentals
  • Understanding drawings (plans, sections & elevations)
  • Units, codes, thumb rules, IS standards
2. Complete Building Estimation
  • Footing, columns, beams, slabs, walls
  • Shuttering, reinforcement, concrete & finishing quantities
  • Complete building measurement using AutoCAD
3. BOQ (Bill of Quantities) Preparation
  • Item description writing
  • Abstract sheet preparation
  • Quantity takeoff formats used in companies
4. Rate Analysis (CPWD/DSR Methods)
  • Labour, material, machinery cost
  • Concrete, plastering, flooring, painting
  • Excel rate analysis templates
5. Bar Bending Schedule (BBS)
  • Cutting lengths of footing, column, beam, slab bars
  • BBS table preparation in Excel
  • Real structural drawings used for practice
6. AutoCAD for Quantity Takeoff
  • Measuring lengths, areas, perimeters & volumes
  • Layers, Xrefs, dimensioning
  • Extracting quantities directly from drawings
7. Excel for Estimation & Costing
  • BOQ formatting
  • Pivot tables, formulas, VLOOKUP
  • Cost sheet preparation
  • Real Projects Included
You will work on:
