What is a Kaizen Event?

A Kaizen Event is a focused, short-term initiative aimed at solving specific problems and achieving rapid, measurable improvements. Unlike long-term improvement projects, Kaizen Events allow cross-functional teams to collaborate intensely over a few days to identify inefficiencies, address root causes, and implement sustainable solutions.

Kaizen Events are the secret behind organizations achieving operational excellence by systematically eliminating waste and streamlining workflows.
