Course Content

About This Udemy Course

The following is the full official course description for Complete PowerPoint and Public Speaking with AI as published on Udemy by instructor Imran Afzal:

Are you ready to learn how to create professional PowerPoint presentations, use AI to work smarter, and speak with confidence in front of any audience?

This course is designed to help you master three essential parts of successful presentations. First, you will learn how to use Microsoft PowerPoint step by step, from beginner to advanced level. Then, you will learn how to use AI tools like Microsoft Copilot and ChatGPT to create presentations faster and more effectively. Finally, you will develop the communication and public speaking skills needed to present your ideas clearly, confidently, and professionally.

Many people know how to open PowerPoint, add a few bullet points, and create basic slides. But far fewer know how to build presentations that truly capture attention, keep the audience engaged, and communicate a message in a clear and memorable way. Even fewer know how to stand in front of an audience and deliver that presentation with confidence.

That is exactly what this course will teach you.

In the first part of the course, you will build a strong foundation in Microsoft PowerPoint. You will learn how to create slides, format text, insert tables, pictures, icons, charts, audio, and video, apply themes and transitions, use animations, manage slide shows, review presentations, work with different views, and understand important file and options settings.

In the new AI section, you will learn how to use AI tools to increase your productivity and improve your presentation workflow. You will explore how Microsoft Copilot in PowerPoint for Microsoft 365 can help you create and improve slide decks, and how ChatGPT can help you brainstorm topics, generate outlines, write slide content, improve structure, prepare speaker notes, and support your presentation practice.

In the final part of the course, you will focus on presentation skills and public speaking. You will learn how to start strong, organize your message, improve body language, connect with your audience, reduce nervousness, answer questions, recover from mistakes, manage time, and deliver presentations that are effective instead of boring.

This is not just a PowerPoint training course. It is a complete presentation and communication course that combines technical slide-building knowledge, modern AI productivity tools, and real-world public speaking techniques.

Most courses teach only one side of the equation. They either teach you how to use PowerPoint or they focus only on public speaking. Very few also show you how AI can support the entire presentation process.

This course combines all three.

You will learn how to use PowerPoint, how to use AI tools like Copilot and ChatGPT to work more efficiently, and how to present your slides in a way that makes people listen, understand, and remember your message.

In this course, you will learn how to:
  • Use Microsoft PowerPoint from beginner to advanced level
  • Create professional slides step by step
  • Edit text, shapes, tables, charts, pictures, audio, and video
  • Apply themes, layouts, transitions, and animations properly
  • Use Microsoft Copilot in PowerPoint to support presentation creation
  • Use ChatGPT to brainstorm topics, create outlines, and improve slide content
  • Use AI to write better titles, bullet points, and speaker notes
  • Build audience-friendly presentations that support your message
  • Improve public speaking skills and presentation confidence
  • Deliver presentations in meetings, classrooms, interviews, webinars, and conferences
  • Keep your audience engaged from the first slide to the last
  • Answer questions with more clarity and control
If you want to learn PowerPoint, improve your public speaking, and use AI to create presentations faster and more effectively, this course is for you.
