About This Course

<div>Discover how to take your .NET applications to the next level by integrating artificial intelligence in a practical, modern, and production-ready way. From there, we’ll move on to Microsoft Extensions AI, one of the key components of the modern .NET ecosystem for working with AI in a flexible and decoupled way, allowing you to switch between providers such as OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google without rewriting your application.</div><div><br></div><div>As we progress, we’ll build a fully functional chatbot using ASP.NET Core. You’ll also learn how to use tool calling, a technique that allows the AI to execute functions from your C# code, integrate with your database, or consume external APIs.</div><div><br></div><div>We’ll also cover more advanced scenarios, such as human-in-the-loop workflows, where the AI requests approval before performing sensitive actions like sending emails. This is essential for building secure and reliable systems in real-world environments.</div><div><br></div><div>In the RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) module, you’ll learn how to build applications that allow AI to answer questions based on your company’s own documents. Not only that, but we’ll also implement a source citation system, increasing the reliability of responses and making it possible to verify the information being used.</div><div><br></div><div>Finally, we’ll explore the Model Context Protocol (MCP), where we’ll build a server that enables tools like ChatGPT or Claude to interact directly with your application. This opens the door to scenarios where users can perform real actions in your system simply through natural language.</div><div><br></div><div>This course is designed for .NET developers who want to learn by building real-world projects they can immediately apply to their jobs or products. You don’t need prior experience with artificial intelligence, but you should have a basic understanding of C# and ASP.NET Core.</div><div><br></div><div>By the end of the course, you won’t just understand how modern AI works, but also you’ll be able to integrate it effectively into your own applications, creating smarter, more automated solutions aligned with today’s industry trends.</div><div><br></div><div>If you want to learn how to build AI and agent-powered .NET applications, this course is for you.</div>

What you'll learn:

  • Integrating AI into .NET applications
  • Build chatbots in ASP.NET Core
  • Enabling AI to call C# functions in your app
  • Integrate AI with external databases and APIs
  • Implement human approval for sensitive actions (make AI ask for permission to do things)
  • Create a RAG system with internal documents (where AI answers questions based on documents)
  • Building and integrating an MCP server
